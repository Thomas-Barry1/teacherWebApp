About TeachGenie

TeachGenie was designed to help teachers save time, reduce stress, and improve the quality of their materials.

Why Choose TeachGenie?

Being a teacher often means creating tons and tons of materials for students daily, on top of having to grade all the tests, homeworks, and quizzes they turn in. And there's almost no time to do this in school, so most of grading and material creation is done on your own time at home. What if there was a way to save time, reduce stress, and take back your after work life? That's where TeachGenie comes in, our ultimate goal is helping teachers, and saving you time along the way. You can invest that time in yourselves.

  • Generate high-quality lesson plans tailored to your needs.
  • Create tests and quizzes effortlessly.
  • Access 24/7 AI assistance for instant support.
